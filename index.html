about me

I am second year engineering student at the University of British Colombia. I am pursuing a major in Electrical engineering and a dual minor in both business and Mechanical engineering. I am very keen on dismantling, understanding and improving technology and I have a very deep passion for both cutting edge design and technology. The experience and ease of implementation must match the level of the functionality of a product or system for me to deem it “functional”. I love to tackle hard problems and find creative and unique solutions.

When I’m not in class or studying I enjoy to spend my time with my hobbies. I’ve recently developed an interest in coffee and have been exploring on different brewing methods, origins and the history of coffee. My other passions include but are not limited to photography, videography, technology, right to repair and travelling. I have the pleasure of calling both Vancouver and Victoria BC home and love to explore both of those cities and take some interesting photos with my various cameras. I shoot both digital, film and instant (DSLR,SLR and Polaroid). I also like to keep up with the automotive world and follow both progress of high end electric and gas powered vehicles.
